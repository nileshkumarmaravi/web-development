missile man of india

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, popularly known as Missile Man of India, was a visionary scientist, educator and statesman who left an indelible mark on India's space and missile programs. APJ Abdul Kalam, born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, India, was a renowned scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a crucial role in India's missile development program and served as a key figure in various scientific organizations. Known as the "People's President," Kalam prioritized education and technology, especially in rural areas. He continued to inspire the youth even after his presidency, through his speeches and writings. Kalam passed away on July 27, 2015, leaving behind a legacy of dedication, innovation, and service to the nation.
